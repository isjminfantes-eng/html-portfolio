The Best Movies According to José Infantes

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ben-Hur

Ben-Hur (1959) American film, epic drama, set in the Roman province of Judea during the reign of Emperor Tiberius. The Roman Empire firmly rules its territories, Judea being one of them, which it oppresses harshly. Its inhabitants yearn for freedom from the Roman yoke.

Main actors: Charlton Heston (Ben-Hur) and Marina Berti (Flavia)


The Poseidon Adventure

The Poseidon Adventure. A 1972 American disaster film. The story of the disaster and sinking of a luxurious, antiquated ocean liner during its final voyage from New York to Athens.

Main cast:
Gene Hackman, Ernest Borgnine, Red Buttons, Shelley Winters, Stella Stevens, Leslie Nielsen, and Roddy McDowall.

Rocky

1976 American film.
The story follows Rocky Balboa, a working-class Italian-American with a talent for boxing, as he pursues the American dream.
